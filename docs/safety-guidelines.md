# Safety Principles and Usage Restrictions

## Expert Consultation System Safety Principles

This system strictly adheres to the following medical safety principles:

### ⚠️ Safety Red Lines (Things We Absolutely Will Not Do)

1. **Do not provide specific medication dosages**
   - The system will not recommend specific drug dosages
   - It will not issue prescriptions or recommend prescription drugs

2. **Do not directly prescribe drug names**
   - It will not replace a physician in writing prescriptions
   - Drug-related information is for reference only

3. **Do not make life-or-death prognoses**
   - It will not predict disease progression or survival time
   - It will not make judgments about life prognosis

4. **Do not replace physician diagnosis**
   - All analyses are for reference only
   - They do not serve as the basis for medical diagnosis

### ✅ What the System Can Do

1. **Interpret the clinical significance of medical test results**
   - Explain what each result indicator represents
   - Describe what an abnormal indicator may mean

2. **Identify abnormal indicators and potential risks**
   - Flag indicators outside the reference range
   - Alert to potential health risks

3. **Provide healthy lifestyle advice**
   - Diet, exercise, and daily routine recommendations
   - Health guidance for disease prevention

4. **Recommend targeted examinations**
   - Suggest items for further testing
   - Recommend relevant clinical departments to visit

5. **Assist in developing follow-up plans**
   - Recommend re-examination frequency
   - Remind users to monitor key indicators

6. **Integrate multidisciplinary expert opinions**
   - Provide comprehensive analysis from multiple perspectives
   - Help users understand opinions from various specialties

### ⚠️ Important Disclaimer

- **All analysis reports from this system are for reference only**
- **They do not serve as the basis for medical diagnosis**
- **All clinical decisions require consultation with a qualified physician**
- **In case of emergency, seek medical attention immediately**

## Usage Restrictions

### System Limitations

1. **Not suitable for medical emergencies**
   - In case of emergency, call emergency services immediately or go to a hospital
   - This system cannot handle acute medical events

2. **Not suitable for children and pregnant women**
   - The system is currently designed primarily for adults
   - Reference standards differ for children and pregnant women

3. **Not suitable for diagnosing serious diseases**
   - Should not be used to confirm serious diagnoses
   - All diagnoses must be made by a qualified physician

### Data Accuracy Limitations

1. **Examination report recognition accuracy**
   - Depends on image quality and clarity
   - Blurry or incomplete images may cause recognition errors

2. **Limitations of reference ranges**
   - Reference ranges may vary by laboratory, equipment, and method
   - Use the reference range from the patient's own hospital as the standard

3. **Estimation of radiation doses**
   - Radiation doses are estimated values
   - Actual doses are affected by many factors including equipment and parameters
   - For health management reference only

### Disclaimer of Liability

1. **For reference only**
   - All information and analyses provided by the system are for reference only
   - They do not constitute medical advice or diagnosis

2. **Professional medical advice**
   - All medical decisions should be made in consultation with a qualified physician
   - Do not rely solely on this system for health decisions

3. **Data security**
   - Users are responsible for their own data backups
   - The system bears no responsibility for data loss

4. **Risk of use**
   - Any consequences arising from the use of this system are borne by the user
   - Developers assume no liability for any losses resulting from the use of this system

## Recommended Usage

### Correct Use

1. **As a health management support tool**
   - Record and track health data
   - Understand trends in health indicator changes

2. **As a preparation tool before medical visits**
   - Organize examination results before a clinic visit
   - Prepare questions to ask the physician

3. **As a health learning tool**
   - Learn about medical laboratory knowledge
   - Understand the clinical significance of various indicators

4. **As a patient-physician communication aid**
   - Better understand physician recommendations
   - Participate in personal health management decisions

### Improper Use (Please Avoid)

1. **Do not rely solely on the system for diagnosis**
   - A qualified physician must always be consulted

2. **Do not self-medicate or adjust medications on your own**
   - All medication use must follow physician instructions

3. **Do not delay seeking medical care**
   - Seek medical attention promptly if symptoms persist or worsen

4. **Do not become overly anxious**
   - Minor fluctuations in indicators are normal
   - A physician should determine whether treatment is needed

## Handling Medical Emergencies

If you experience any of the following, seek medical attention immediately or call emergency services:

- Chest pain or chest tightness
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe headache
- Altered consciousness
- Serious trauma
- Allergic reaction
- Other urgent symptoms
