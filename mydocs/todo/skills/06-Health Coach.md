# Wellness Coach Skill Design

## Overview
**Skill Name**: `wellness-coach`
**Purpose**: Provide personalized health guidance, lifestyle recommendations, and behavioral coaching based on comprehensive health data analysis.

## Description
Act as a personal health coach, providing lifestyle recommendations, goal-setting help, habit formation guidance, and motivation based on personal health data. Use when seeking health advice, lifestyle changes, or asking "How should I improve my health?"

## Data Integration

### Data Sources
- **Personal Profile**: Current health status
- **Symptom Records**: How lifestyle affects symptoms
- **Mood Records**: Mental health patterns
- **Diet Records**: Nutritional habits
- **Sleep Records**: Sleep quality
- **Exercise Records**: Physical activity
- **Health Goals**: Personal health objectives

### Related Skills
- All skills provide data for coach recommendations
- `health-insights`: Provides the foundation for coaching

## Core Features

### 1. Personalized Recommendations
- **Diet & Nutrition**: Meal planning, nutrition guidance
- **Exercise & Fitness**: Activity recommendations, workout plans
- **Sleep Hygiene**: Sleep improvement strategies
- **Stress Management**: Stress reduction techniques
- **Lifestyle Optimization**: Daily life improvements

### 2. Goal Setting & Tracking
- **SMART Goals**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
- **Goal Breakdown**: Break large goals into manageable steps
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor goal achievement
- **Adjustment Guidance**: Modify goals based on progress

### 3. Habit Formation
- **Habit Design**: Create sustainable healthy habits
- **Trigger Identification**: Understand habit triggers
- **Habit Stacking**: Build new habits on top of existing ones
- **Habit Tracking**: Monitor habit consistency

### 4. Motivation & Accountability
- **Progress Recognition**: Celebrate wins and milestones
- **Encouragement**: Provide motivation during challenges
- **Accountability Check-ins**: Regular check-ins
- **Obstacle Planning**: Anticipate and overcome barriers

## Coaching Philosophy

### Principles
1. **Progress over perfection**: Consistent small wins > occasional perfection
2. **Personalization**: One size doesn't fit all
3. **Sustainability**: Build lasting habits, not quick fixes
4. **Empathy**: Setbacks are learning opportunities, not failures
5. **Evidence-based**: Science-backed recommendations
6. **Holistic approach**: Focus on the whole person, not just symptoms

### Behavioral Science Techniques
- **Habit Stacking**: Attach new habits to existing ones
- **Implementation Intentions**: "When X happens, I will do Y"
- **Progressive Overload**: Gradually increase difficulty
- **Social Support**: Leverage accountability partners
- **Reward Systems**: Celebrate milestones and wins
- **Cognitive Reframing**: Reframe negative thoughts
